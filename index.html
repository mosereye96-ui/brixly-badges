30-Day Guarantee

Reviews don't grow? You get your money back.

We stand behind every badge. If your monthly Google reviews haven't grown by at least 15% after a full 30 days of wearing your Brixly badge, you're entitled to a full refund.

  1. Wear and use your badge consistently for at least 30 days after delivery.
  2. Track your review count before and after — a few screenshots or your Google Business Profile analytics are all you need.
  3. If the 15% increase doesn't show up, send us your before/after numbers along with your order details.
  4. We'll review your claim and respond promptly. Approved refunds are issued within 14 business days.
  5. Return shipping on the badge itself is covered by the customer.
Questions

Before you order.

What happens to my logo and business info once I send it?
It's used only to design and program your badge. We don't share it with anyone else, and it's not used for anything beyond producing your order.
How long does it take to receive my badge?
Most orders are designed, printed, and shipped within a week of us receiving your details.
Does it work with any phone?
Yes — it works with any NFC-enabled smartphone (which covers the vast majority of phones made in the last several years). No app required.
Can I change the design after I order?
Reach out before we print and we're happy to make adjustments. Once printed, changes require a new order.
